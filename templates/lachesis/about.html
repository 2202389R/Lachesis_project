About Page

The purpose of our application is to provide a platform where short story writers connect with their readers.

The application will enable writer to provide choices for their readers at specific points in their story. The readers will then be given a time period in which they can vote on what direction they desire the story to take. When the voting period ends, the writer will then continue the story according to the most popular choice unitl another reader decision has to be made.

When a writer starts a story they will be able to set a time interval which users should expect the story to be updated. They will also be able to set the duration of the voting period which start whenever they add a story segment.
